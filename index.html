About Us

Capstone Job Portal is a modern employment platform designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers. Our mission is to provide easy access to verified job opportunities while ensuring a smooth, user-friendly experience. With powerful search, quick apply features, and responsive design, we aim to make your career journey effortless and effective. This job portal is built as a capstone project to showcase how technology can simplify the hiring process. It allows users to explore openings, apply online, and connect with employers quickly. The platform demonstrates real-world features of popular job websites while being lightweight, responsive, and student-friendly.
